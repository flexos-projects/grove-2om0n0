<flex_block type="instructions">
The context collection is the default catch-all for information that doesn't
fit a structured collection. Drop anything here:
- Company history and mission
- Team bios
- Brand voice guidelines
- Competitor research
- Marketing copy drafts
- Client feedback
- Random notes

The AI reads these when generating specs, prototypes, and builds
to stay aligned with the project's identity.
</flex_block>
