# About Grove

Grove is an SMS concierge platform for boutique hotels. Guests text a phone number — printed on the room key card, the welcome letter, the nightstand card — and get anything they need handled: dinner reservations, late checkout, taxi to the airport, local recommendations, or help with a room issue. No app to download, no QR code to scan. Just a text message.

Behind the scenes, Grove's AI processes requests instantly. Simple requests (local recommendations, directions, hotel information) get natural-language responses in the hotel's own voice. Complex or emotional requests get routed to hotel staff through a clean operator dashboard. The guest never knows which is which.

Grove is sold to hotel operators as a B2B SaaS product. The guest-facing interface is SMS only — there is no guest app. The "product" that hotel staff interact with is the operator dashboard: an inbox for conversations, guest profiles with preference memory, request routing, quick actions, analytics, and multi-property management.

## Who It's For

- **Primary:** Boutique hotel operators (owners/GMs of 15-80 room independent hotels or small groups of 2-8 properties) who care deeply about guest experience and want technology that meets their taste standard.
- **Secondary:** Hotel staff (front desk, concierge) who use the dashboard daily and need something faster than their current workflow.
- **Tertiary:** Hotel guests, who never see the product — only SMS. The interaction should feel warmer and faster than walking to the front desk.

## Voice & Tone

Discreet but present. Warm but never familiar. Competent but never robotic. Confident but never loud. Considered.

The product should feel like the lobby of the hotel it serves — calm, intentional, with nothing out of place. Information density of Linear, but with the warmth of natural materials.

## Key Facts

- **Business model:** B2B SaaS — hotels subscribe, not guests
- **Guest interface:** SMS only (no app download)
- **AI approach:** Hybrid — AI handles ~70-80% of requests, humans handle the rest with full context
- **Brand colour:** Deep forest green — not as an accent, as the identity
- **Tech stack:** Nuxt 4, Convex, Vercel, Trigger.dev v4
- **Integrations planned:** Twilio (SMS), Stripe (billing), Resend (email), PMS integrations (Mews, Cloudbeds, Little Hotelier) as growth features
- **Target market:** Independent boutique hotels in US, UK, and EU
- **Not for:** Hotel chains (100+ properties), hostels/budget, vacation rentals, self-service app seekers
