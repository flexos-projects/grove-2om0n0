# Initial Brief Interpreted

- Updated: spaces/001-kickoff/space.md

## Intent

"A concierge app for boutique hotels — guests text a number and get everything sorted. Think of it as a butler in your pocket. Our brand colour is deep forest green."

## Interpretation

Positioned as a hospitality-grade SMS concierge platform — not a tech product that happens to serve hotels, but a hotel product that happens to use technology. The "butler" metaphor and forest green anchor drove a direction rooted in discretion, warmth, and quiet competence. Named the project "Grove" — evokes the forest green, implies shelter and cultivation, and sounds like a place rather than a platform.
