<flex_block type="instructions">
Vision specs capture the project's identity and direction.
Often small — a paragraph or two — capturing what the founder says in passing
that defines who they are, what they want, and what matters.

SUBTYPES:
- goal: What the project aims to achieve. Mission, objectives, success metrics.
- constraint: Hard limits. Budget, timeline, regulatory, accessibility.
- audience: Who uses this. Personas, demographics, user segments.
- brand: Identity, voice, personality, visual direction (not tokens — those go in design/).

NAMING: NNN-{subtype}-{slug}.md
EXAMPLE: 001-goal-core-mission.md, 002-audience-primary-users.md

STATUS: roadmap (planned), active (current), archived (no longer relevant).
</flex_block>
