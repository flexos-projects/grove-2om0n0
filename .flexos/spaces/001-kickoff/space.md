# Project Kickoff

The founder described an SMS-based concierge service for boutique hotels — guests text a number and get everything handled, from dinner reservations to airport transfers. The creative direction leans into the hospitality world rather than the tech world: deep forest green, editorial typography, and the quiet confidence of a well-run hotel lobby. The product serves two audiences — guests who never see the product (only SMS) and hotel operators who need a dashboard that meets the standard of taste they apply to everything else in their property.
