## Page Specifications

### Landing Page (`/`)

**Purpose:** Convert hotel operators from visitor to signup. Single page, no navigation maze.

**Audience:** Boutique hotel owners/managers who found Grove through word of mouth, search, or a referral. Likely on mobile (checking it while at work).

**Sections (top to bottom):**

1. **Hero** — Headline, sub-headline, single CTA ("Start Free Trial"), and a product screenshot showing the inbox with a guest conversation. No video. No animation. Clean.
2. **How It Works** — 3-step illustration: (1) Guest texts your number, (2) AI handles or routes to staff, (3) Guest is delighted. Simple icons, short captions.
3. **For Hotels That Care** — Prose section. 2-3 paragraphs about why boutique hotels deserve better tools. Emotional, not feature-listy.
4. **Features Overview** — 6 feature cards in a grid: SMS Concierge, AI Responses, Guest Memory, Request Routing, Knowledge Base, Analytics. Icon + headline + one-line each.
5. **Social Proof** — 2-3 testimonial quotes from hotel operators (placeholder for v1, real once live). Property name + title.
6. **Pricing Preview** — 3 plan cards (Starter, Professional, Group) with prices and top 3 features each. "See Full Pricing" link.
7. **Final CTA** — "Give your guests the concierge they deserve." + signup button.
8. **Footer** — Links, legal, contact email.

**Key interactions:**
- CTA buttons → `/signup`
- Pricing link → `/pricing`
- Smooth scroll between sections

**Empty/loading/error states:** N/A — static page.

### Signup / Onboarding (`/signup`)

**Purpose:** Create account and configure first property in 4 steps, under 5 minutes.

**Audience:** Hotel operator who just clicked "Start Free Trial."

**Steps:**

1. **Account** — Email, password, full name. "Create Account" button.
2. **Property** — Property name, address (autocomplete), timezone (auto-detected), brief description.
3. **Phone Number** — Select area code preference, system provisions a Twilio number. Preview the number. "This is the number guests will text."
4. **First Taste** — Preview of what a guest conversation looks like. "Send yourself a test text" button. Shows the AI greeting in action.

**Key interactions:**
- Step progress indicator (1/4, 2/4, etc.)
- Back navigation between steps
- Skip step 4 (but discouraged with microcopy)
- After completion → redirect to `/inbox` with a welcome overlay

**Error states:**
- Email already registered → inline error with link to login
- Phone provisioning fails → retry with different area code, or manual support contact

### Inbox (`/inbox`)

**Purpose:** The primary workspace. Where staff spend 90% of their time. Conversation list on the left, active conversation on the right.

**Audience:** All authenticated staff and admins.

**Layout:** Two-panel (desktop). Left: conversation list (320px). Right: conversation thread + response input. On mobile: list view, tap to enter conversation, back button to return.

**Left Panel — Conversation List:**
- Search bar at top (searches guest name, phone, message content)
- Filter tabs: All | Active | Waiting | Resolved
- Each conversation row: guest name/phone, last message preview (truncated), timestamp, status dot (green=active, yellow=waiting, grey=resolved), assignee avatar, AI badge if last response was AI, unread indicator
- Sorted by most recent activity
- New conversation badge for first-time guests

**Right Panel — Conversation Thread:**
- Guest name/phone at top with link to guest profile
- Message bubbles: guest messages left-aligned (light bg), staff/AI responses right-aligned (green bg for staff, subtle green with AI badge for AI)
- Internal notes: yellow background, "Staff Only" label, cannot be accidentally sent
- Timestamps between message groups
- Scroll to load older messages

**Response Area (bottom of right panel):**
- Text input field (multi-line, expands)
- AI draft suggestion bar above input (dismissable, editable, one-tap to use)
- Quick action chips above input
- Send button
- Internal note toggle (switches input to note mode)
- Attachment button (image upload)
- Character count / SMS segment indicator

**Key interactions:**
- Click conversation → loads in right panel (desktop) or navigates (mobile)
- Assign: click assignee avatar → dropdown of team members
- Resolve: button in conversation header → moves to Resolved filter
- Reopen: button on resolved conversations
- Keyboard: Enter to send, Shift+Enter for newline, Cmd+K for quick actions

**Empty state:** No conversations yet → "Your inbox is empty. Share your Grove number with guests to get started. Your number: (555) 123-4567" with a copy button.

**Loading state:** Skeleton conversation list, skeleton message bubbles.

### Guest Directory (`/guests`)

**Purpose:** Browse and search all guests who have ever texted the hotel.

**Audience:** Staff and admins.

**Sections:**
- Search bar (name, phone number)
- Guest list: name, phone, last contact date, total conversations, preference tags
- Sort: most recent, alphabetical, most conversations
- Click → guest profile page

**Empty state:** "No guests yet. When guests text your Grove number, they'll appear here."

### Guest Profile (`/guests/:id`)

**Purpose:** Everything the hotel knows about one guest.

**Audience:** Staff and admins.

**Sections:**
1. **Header** — Name (editable), phone number, first contact date, total conversations
2. **Preferences** — AI-extracted tags (editable). E.g., "High floor", "Shellfish allergy", "Early riser", "Likes Italian food"
3. **Staff Notes** — Free text notes from staff (markdown supported)
4. **Conversation History** — Grouped by visit. Each visit shows date range and message count. Expandable to see full conversation.

**Key interactions:**
- Edit name inline
- Add/remove preference tags
- Add staff notes
- Click conversation → opens in inbox

### Knowledge Base (`/knowledge`)

**Purpose:** Build and manage the local information the AI uses to answer guest questions.

**Audience:** Staff and admins.

**Sections:**
- Category tabs: All | Dining | Nightlife | Activities | Shopping | Transport | Services | Hotel FAQ
- Entry cards: title, category badge, short description, link icon if URL present
- Add Entry button → modal form (title, category, description, optional URL, optional hours, seasonal toggle)
- Search bar
- Entry count / limit indicator (Starter tier)

**Key interactions:**
- Add entry → modal form → save
- Edit entry → same modal, pre-filled
- Delete entry → confirmation dialog
- Bulk import → paste text → AI parses into entries → review and confirm

**Empty state:** "No entries yet. Add your favourite local restaurants, attractions, and tips so the AI can recommend them to guests." + "Add First Entry" button.

### Analytics Dashboard (`/analytics`)

**Purpose:** Understand how Grove is performing. Metrics that matter for hospitality.

**Audience:** Admins only.

**Sections:**
1. **Metric Cards (top)** — Total conversations, AI-handled %, average response time, guest satisfaction score. Each with trend arrow (vs previous period).
2. **Conversations Over Time** — Line chart, selectable period (7d, 30d, 90d, custom).
3. **Request Categories** — Pie/donut chart. Dining, Transport, Housekeeping, Activities, Information, Complaint, Other.
4. **Response Time Distribution** — Bar chart. <1min, 1-5min, 5-15min, 15min+.
5. **Busiest Hours** — Heat map. Day of week × hour.
6. **Staff Performance** — Table: staff member, conversations handled, avg response time, satisfaction score.

**Key interactions:**
- Period selector affects all charts
- Hover for tooltips on charts
- Export CSV button

**Empty state:** "Not enough data yet. Analytics will appear after your first 50 conversations."
