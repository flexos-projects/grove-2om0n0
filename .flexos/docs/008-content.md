>
> [Accept Invitation] (button)
>

**Tone:** Warm, brief. Not corporate. The invitee may not know what Grove is — the email explains just enough.

> **Waiting:** {duration}
> **Last message:** "{messagePreview}"

**Tone:** Informative, not pushy. State the fact, offer the option, move on.
